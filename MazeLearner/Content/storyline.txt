Title: Maze Learner

Premise
A magical maze appears at school. A Grade 5 student must navigate it, answer English questions from guardians,
 and rescue the class pet Tala.

Intro:
Year 1629, The planet earth are inhabitant of different races, humans, elf, dwarfes and many more, these races live in harmony and peace
suddenly a dark forces arrive trembling all nation of races and destorying there homes and everything they have, the war last for 30 years
so many people fallen in war and one day a hero arrive, with the hero's subordinate they able to fight back with the dark forces and the
ended the war, but the hero fade before he fall the hero use last resort sealing everyone with different realms.

Characters
- Player (choose Mira or Jao)  
- Tala (class carabao)  
- Mazekeeper (final guardian)  
- Area Guardians (ask questions)


Story Progression

Core loop
1. Walk the maze.  
2. Meet a guardians.  
3. Answer  English questions.  
4. Correct → proceed; wrong → small penalty (slow or detour).  
5. Find keys/map to reach Tala.

Skills (Grade 5)
- Vocabulary (meanings, synonyms/antonyms)  
- Reading comprehension (short passages)  
- Grammar (tenses, subject-verb agreement)  
- Spelling (choose correct word)  
- Sentence order (rearrange words)

Maze layout
1. Entrance — tutorial, 1 easy vocab question  
2. Library
3. Market 
4. River 
5. Shadow Corridor
6. Tala’s Pen - final
